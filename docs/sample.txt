# Company Policy Document - TechCorp Inc.

## Leave Policy
Every employee gets 20 paid leave days per year.
Sick leave is separate and unlimited with a doctor's note.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next year.

## Work From Home Policy
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week.
Managers must approve WFH requests at least 1 day in advance.
All employees must be available during core hours: 10 AM to 4 PM.

## Salary Policy
Salaries are reviewed every 6 months.
Performance bonuses are paid in March and September.
New employees are eligible for a bonus after 6 months of service.
